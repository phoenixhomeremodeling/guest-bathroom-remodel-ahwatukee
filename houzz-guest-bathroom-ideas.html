Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most cost effective shower remodel?

Choose a standard size prefabricated shower, with acrylic and cultured marble being the typically more budget-friendly prefab materials, or if opting for a custom shower, opt for simplicity, as white porcelain subway tile is a more affordable option compared to natural stone while still offering durability.

What is the best color for a guest bathroom?

To establish a tranquil, spa-like atmosphere in your bathroom, consider using calming neutral shades such as blue, green, and gray, drawing inspiration from nature with hues like soft sage, sky blue, and ash gray, or, for a cozier ambiance, opt for warmer tones like pale yellow, warm beige, or a subtle blush color to impart a welcoming radiance to the space.

What is the least expensive way to install a shower?

To create an affordable shower, opt for a straightforward design utilizing cost-effective materials, such as pre-fabricated shower kits, acrylic or fiberglass walls, basic plumbing fixtures, a shower curtain instead of an enclosure, and standard tiles or a basic shower base.
